What is Case Conversion?

Case conversion is the process of changing the capitalization of text to fit a specific format or standard. Whether you need to format titles for blog posts, convert variables for coding, or fix accidentally capitalized sentences, a reliable case converter saves you time and ensures consistency.

What does Toggle Case do?

Toggle Case reverses the capitalization of every single letter. Uppercase letters become lowercase, and vice versa. It's often used to fix text typed with Caps Lock accidentally turned on.

Does it store my text?

No. All conversions happen entirely in your browser. We do not transmit or store your text on our servers. Your Case History is stored locally on your device for your convenience.

How does Title Case work?

Title Case capitalizes the first letter of most words, while keeping minor words (like 'a', 'the', 'in') lowercase unless they are the first word, adhering to standard publishing guidelines.
